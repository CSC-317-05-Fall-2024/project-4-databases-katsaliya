The Grand Palace

The Grand Palace is a stunning complex of buildings that once served as the official residence of Thai kings. Wat Phra Kaew, located within the Grand Palace, is home to the revered Emerald Buddha.

Wat Arun

Also known as the Temple of Dawn, this riverside temple is famous for its tall spires and stunning views of the Chao Phraya River, especially at sunset.

Phi Phi Islands

A group of islands known for their dramatic limestone cliffs, clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life. Maya Bay on Phi Phi Leh became famous after being featured in the movie "The Beach."
